WE SERVE LABS

Altium companies serve Laboratories with Analyses and Methods, Instrumentation, Design & Installation, Consumables & Supplies, Service & Maintenance and Trainings.

Altium provides a range of solutions for customers to optimize their laboratory analyses and methods. This includes offering innovative laboratory equipment and technology, as well as providing expert advice and guidance on the best practices and methods to achieve accurate and reliable results.

Industries

Altium provides laboratory solutions to industries such as pharmaceuticals, food, energy, environment, medicine, healthcare, and green energy. Their solutions include testing for contaminants, research and development, clinical diagnostics, and analytical methods tailored to the specific needs of each industry.

Pharma and Biopharma

In the pharma and biopharma industry, Altium provides laboratory solutions for drug discovery, development, and quality control. This includes analytical methods such as high-performance liquid chromatography (HPLC), mass spectrometry (MS), as well as cell culture and molecular biology techniques.

Food

In the food industry, Altium provides laboratory solutions for food safety and quality control. This includes testing for contaminants such as pesticides, heavy metals, and microbiological agents, as well as analysis of food composition, nutrition, and flavor. Altium also offers solutions for food packaging and shelf-life testing.

Energy

In the energy industry, Altium provides laboratory solutions for research and development of new energy technologies, as well as quality control of fuels and lubricants. This includes analysis of fuel properties such as octane number, cetane number, and flash point, as well as testing of lubricant viscosity, wear resistance, and oxidative stability.

Environment

In the environmental industry, Altium provides laboratory solutions for monitoring and analysis of air, water, and soil quality. This includes testing for contaminants such as heavy metals, organic compounds, and pathogens, as well as analysis of environmental parameters such as pH, temperature, and dissolved oxygen.

Medicine and Healthcare

In the medicine and healthcare industry, Altium provides laboratory solutions for clinical diagnostics, drug development, and research. This includes analysis of biological samples such as blood, urine, and tissue, as well as techniques such as PCR, ELISA, and flow cytometry.

Green Energy

In the green energy industry, Altium provides laboratory solutions for research and development of renewable energy technologies. This includes analysis of materials such as solar cells, batteries, and fuel cells, as well as testing of energy storage and conversion systems.
