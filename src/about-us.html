About Us

Trading company Altium International doo is an authorized distributor of analytical equipment of Agilent Technologies for Croatia, Bosnia and Herzegovina and Kosovo, an authorized distributor of Ortho Clinical Diagnostics for Croatia and other instruments and equipment of a number of leading global companies for the above markets.

Agilent Technologies is a leading manufacturer of analytical equipment in the field of natural sciences, diagnostics and applied chemistry. Ortho Clinical Diagnostics has one of the leading positions in the field of transfusion medicine, clinical chemistry and immunodiagnostics.

Altium International in Croatia and Bosnia and Herzegovina employs 57 highly educated employees in sales, administration and maintenance who are authorized to work with instruments from Agilent Technologies and other manufacturers whose instruments it distributes.
